Here you can search for movies and shows, or click the See Trending button to see what is currently trending. Click the hamburger menu in the top left to change theme and navigate to your list. Swipe horizontally to view the search results. Click on a movie or show to see more information about them, including where they are streaming.

Slider description

The trending screen will show the top 10 trending movies and shows. This list is updated daily. Click the button in the top right to toggle the scroll direction between vertical and horizontal.

See where the movie or show is available for streaming, renting and purchasing by choosing from the dropdown. You can also change region, the app will remember the region you used last. Click the icon on the streaming provider to open the app. Save/remove the movie or show to/from your list by clicking the red button.
